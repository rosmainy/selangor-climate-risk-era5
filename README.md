## üìä Executive Summary
This system establishes a climatic baseline for Selangor by processing hourly data points from 2020 to 2025 using **ERA5-Land Reanalysis**.

**Key Insight:** The data reveals a distinct upward trend in rainfall intensity in **2024**. By overlaying this with our Astronomical and Turath modules, we aim to predict **Compound Risk Events** (Rain + Tide + Storm Surge).

### üåßÔ∏è The Concept: Non-Mechanical RF Sensing
Unlike traditional "Tipping Bucket" gauges that are prone to debris blockage and mechanical failure, our prototype utilizes **Advanced RF Signal Attenuation Technology**.

*   **The Innovation:** We move away from mechanical measurement to **Atmospheric Density Sensing**.
*   **The Capabilities:**
    *   **Instant Detection:** Detects rainfall intensity the moment it starts (t=0), unlike physical gauges that need water to accumulate first.
    *   **Zero-Maintenance:** No moving parts, suitable for deployment in remote or harsh urban environments.
*   **The Value:** Provides a critical **15-30 minute lead time** for flash flood warnings before water levels rise on the ground.
